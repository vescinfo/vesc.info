1. USB and Stlink

Most VESC(if not all) have a USB port and CANBUS transceiver
but, several other methods exist to connect a VESC wirelessly to an instance of VESC-TOOL. The newest and greatest way is using VESC-Express, an ESP32-c3 based dongle, comparatively to the nrf51 based bluetooth modules it offers faster speeds and is more extensible.
